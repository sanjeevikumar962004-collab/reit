Answers

Frequently Asked Questions.

What IT services do you offer?
Stackly IT Solutions provides end-to-end technology services including custom software development, cloud infrastructure migration, advanced cybersecurity, network management, and 24/7 managed IT support.
How do you ensure data security?
We deploy multi-layered cybersecurity protocols including endpoint detection, zero-trust architecture, automated encrypted backups, and regular vulnerability audits to ensure your sensitive business data is completely protected.
Do you offer 24/7 support?
Yes, our Professional and Enterprise plans include 24/7 priority help desk support and continuous network monitoring to ensure maximum uptime and immediate incident response.
How long does a cloud migration take?
The timeline depends entirely on the size and complexity of your legacy systems. A basic email and file migration can take a few days, whereas a full infrastructure migration to AWS or Azure might take several weeks. We provide a precise timeline after our initial technical audit.
